OUR TECHNIQUES

Our Glass Techniques

01

Églomisé Glass

Gold, silver, and metal leaf are applied to the reverse of the glass. The finish plays with light and reflection, from subtle patina to bold, gilded brilliance.

02

Hand Etched Glass

Fine engraving tools cut directly into the glass surface. This precise, controlled method captures delicate detail while giving each piece a permanent, hand-crafted character.

03

Edge-Lit Glass

Edge-lit glass uses etched or carved details illuminated by hidden LEDs, causing the designs to glow with depth and clarity. The light enhances contrast and movement, creating vivid, striking panels.

04

Sand Blasted Glass

Compressed air propels fine abrasive particles against the glass surface, creating textured, frosted effects. This technique allows for precise pattern creation and varying degrees of opacity.
